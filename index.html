Euxo

App Information

Euxo is a simple utility app intended to help musicians create sequenced loops with Euclidean rhythms.

These rhythms are created by spreading their 'on' notes as equally as possible across the length of a loop, with intervals equalling the 'greatest common denominator' between the number of steps and the number of desired notes.

This is not a challenge when steps and notes are easily divisible into whole numbers, but becomes much more difficult when they don't.

Use Euxo to do the complicated math for you, and let it show you how to spread your chosen number of notes across the number of steps in your loop.

Euxo does not make any sound. Does not connect to sequencers. And does not read music. It just displays 'X's to indicate steps needing a note, and 'O's for intervals. You just need to tap those into your own sequencer-based instrument.

The maximum number of steps this version of Euxo will work with is 16, but who knows, a future version may attempt more!
